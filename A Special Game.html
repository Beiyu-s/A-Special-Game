A Special Journey

Help the pixel hero collect emojis, defeat enemies, and unlock special surprises in this black and white fairy tale adventure!

Made with ❤️ for TOMMY

HOW TO PLAY

Keyboard Controls:

← → - Move left/right

↑ - Jump

Space - Shoot hearts

P - Pause game

Touch Controls:

Use virtual joystick to move and jump

Tap buttons to shoot and pause

Game Objectives:

Collect emojis and defeat enemies

Collect 10 roses to activate shield

Reach score 1000 or level 27 to win

Controls

  • ← → - Move
